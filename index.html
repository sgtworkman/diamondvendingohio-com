Yes — completely free. We provide the machine, deliver it, install it, keep it stocked, and handle any repairs. You never pay a penny. We make money from the product sales, and you get a great amenity for your location at no cost.

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed within 24–48 hours of your request. We'll coordinate a convenient time for your business and handle everything from delivery to setup.

What if the machine breaks or needs service?

Just call or text us. We respond fast and handle all repairs at no cost to you. Our goal is to make sure the machine is always working and fully stocked.

Can I choose what products go in my machine?

Absolutely. We work with you to stock products your employees and customers actually want. We're flexible — if something isn't selling, we swap it out for something better.

Do you offer ATMs for small businesses?

Yes — and you can earn a share of ATM transaction fees, turning your location into a passive revenue source. We handle the machine, cash management, and all maintenance.

What's the minimum size location you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes — from small offices and waiting rooms to large warehouses and retail stores. If you have foot traffic, we can find a solution that works.
